Other articles

  • Four outreach articles in Spanish about the history, the mathematical formalization, and some experimental possibilities of the twelve-tone system, a musical composition technique from the 20th century. Published on the online journal divulgaMAT, from the Real Sociedad Matemática Española / RSME, 2019.
    • Serialismo y matemáticas (I): historical introduction, mathematical postulates, and analysis of an example work.
    • Serialismo y matemáticas (II): the dihedral group, clock diagrams, and integral serialism.
    • Serialismo y matemáticas (III): group actions, Burnside's lemma, counting of the serial spectrum, and counting of n-gons.
    • Re-escalando música: the twelve-tone artistic vision, euclidean scales, and reduction of dissonance experiments.
  • akesi li kama, a sci-fi short story in Spanish and toki pona. Published on the Sci·Fdi journal, 2022.
