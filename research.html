Other articles

  • 2022: akesi li kama, a sci-fi short story in Spanish and toki pona, on the Sci·Fdi journal.
  • 2019: Four outreach articles in Spanish about the mathematical formalization of the twelve-tone music composition system, on the divulgaMAT journal.
    • Serialismo y matemáticas (I): historical introduction and mathematical postulates.
    • Serialismo y matemáticas (II): the dihedral group, clock diagrams, and integral serialism.
    • Serialismo y matemáticas (III): group actions, Burnside's lemma, and enumeration of serial spectra and n-gons.
    • Re-escalando música: the twelve-tone artistic vision, euclidean scales, and reduction of dissonance.
