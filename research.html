Other articles

  • Four outreach articles in Spanish about the mathematical formalization of the twelve-tone music composition system. Published on the divulgaMAT journal, 2019.
    • Serialismo y matemáticas (I): historical introduction and mathematical postulates.
    • Serialismo y matemáticas (II): the dihedral group, clock diagrams, and integral serialism.
    • Serialismo y matemáticas (III): group actions, Burnside's lemma, counting of the serial spectrum, and counting of n-gons.
    • Re-escalando música: the twelve-tone artistic vision, euclidean scales, and reduction of dissonance.
