About me

I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Advanced Computer Science with Artificial Intelligence at the University of Strathclyde, Glasgow, fully funded by a postgraduate fellowship from ”la Caixa” Foundation.

I have two Bachelor's degrees, one in Mathematics and another one in Computer Science, from Universidad Complutense de Madrid.

I am interested in theoretical computer science, especially from a mathematical point of view. I have worked in computational complexity in the past, as well as in mathematical music theory.
