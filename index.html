About me

In October I will start a PhD position at the Relational Machine Learning Lab at the CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security, enrolled at the University of Saarland (Graduate School of Computer Science), and advised by Rebekka Burkholz.

I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Advanced Computer Science with Artificial Intelligence at the University of Strathclyde, fully funded by a postgraduate fellowship from ”la Caixa” Foundation. I have two Bachelor's degrees, one in Mathematics and another one in Computer Science, from Universidad Complutense de Madrid.

In the past, I have enjoyed exploring applied category theory (string diagrams), complexity theory (P vs. NP), and mathematical music theory (the twelve-tone system). I have been a competitive programmer (SWERC), a mathematical olympian (EGMO), a LaTeX package developer, and a Baroque recorder player.
