About me

In October I will start a PhD position at the Relational Machine Learning Lab at the CISPA Helmholtz Center for Information Security, enrolled at the University of Saarland and advised by Rebekka Burkholz.

I have two Bachelor's degrees in Mathematics and Computer Science from Universidad Complutense de Madrid. I have a Master's degree in Advanced Computer Science with Artificial Intelligence at the University of Strathclyde, fully funded by a postgraduate fellowship from ”la Caixa” Foundation.

History

In the past, I have enjoyed exploring complexity theory (P vs. NP), mathematical music theory (the twelve-tone system), and functional programming (Haskell diagrams). I have been a mathematical olympian and organizer at EGMO, a competitive programmer at SWERC, a LaTeX package developer, and a Baroque recorder player.
